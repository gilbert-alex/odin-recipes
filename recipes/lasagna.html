Homemade Lasagna

Pile up layers of goodness and enjoy on a cold fall day.

Ingredients:

  • ½ pound ground pork
  • ½ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tbs chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 ½ tsp dried basil
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • ⅛ tsp white sugar
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F and boil salted pasta water.
  2. Cook the meat: Brown pork and beef, add onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the tomato sauce, half the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 tsps of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  3. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente.
  4. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, and the remaining parsley, salt, and pepper into a bowl.
  5. Assemble the lasagna: Into a baking dish layer meat, noodles, and cheese being careful not to overfill.
  6. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes covered and 5-10 minutes uncovered until the top is golden brown.
